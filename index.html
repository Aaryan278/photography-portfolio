About

I shoot with whatever's on me — usually my phone or old Sony Cybershot. Based in Dublin, I'm drawn to the natural beauty of our world and the bustling noise of the city. I like to capture the quiet moments of nostalgia, the familiar light, and the stories.

I'm an amateur photographer, and this is my personal project. I share my work here for anyone who enjoys nature, nostalgia, and the beauty of everyday life.

Based Dublin, IE
Shoots 35mm, occasional 120
Cameras Samsung A23, Sony Cybershot
